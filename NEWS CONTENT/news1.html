Simone Biles Tennis Volleyball

Simone Biles

Simone Biles is a famous gymnast who claimed a record of eight all-round US championship less than a month after a 2-year break from gymnastics. Biles returned to compete in Chicago earlier this month after her injury and withdrawing from 5 of her 6 finals at the Tokyo Olympics in 2021 because she had to focus on her mental health. Though she still claimed the title of the seven-time Olympic medallist who became the oldest female US champion. Though, she still has not confirmed whether she plans to compete next year at the Olympic Games in Paris.
