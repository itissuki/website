Lucky by Jason Mraz, ft Colbie Caillat

This song was inspired by a chance of meeting with Colbie Caillat during a party in Los Angeles. Mraz was struck by her beauty and talent that he knew he wanted to collaborate with her. Lucky by Jason Mraz, ft Colbie Caillat, capture the feels of a healthy and happy relationship. The song showed how lucky the two lovers are to have found each other. It also shows the joy and happiness that come from being in love. The song is about finding the perfect person to share your life with and gives the sense of happiness. It also shows the importance of appreciating the people in our lives who makes us feel loved and supported. This song is a reminder of the beauty and power of love and to cherish people that makes our lives better. The meaning behind this song is how two people can come together even when they are far apart. Love and connection.
